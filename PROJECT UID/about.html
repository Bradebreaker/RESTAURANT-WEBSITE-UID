Our Story

From a small kitchen to Tuticorin's favorite chicken spot

Started in 2020

CRISPY began as a small takeaway counter near VOC College in 2020, with just one secret recipe for fried chicken. Through word-of-mouth and our commitment to quality, we've grown to 3 locations across Tuticorin, serving over 10,000 happy customers every month.
